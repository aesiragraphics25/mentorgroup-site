Our greatest reward will be to present to society, our young women that are fearfully and wonderfully made. About Us

Ambassadors and Teens with Wings mentoring program was established in April of 2015 in Mobile, Alabama. ATW is a faith built non-profit organization dedicated to educating young women on how to face the challenges of today. Our mission is to empower these young women with confidence, power, and the knowledge to become sucessful leaders, regardless of their background or circumstances. Our focus is on self-esteem, values, honesty, respect, responsibility, and faith.
